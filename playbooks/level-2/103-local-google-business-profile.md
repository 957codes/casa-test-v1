# Local Google Business Profile and Maps

For a local service, the map pack is the storefront. Most customers find you here, not on
a website.

## Procedure

1. Claim and verify the Google Business Profile for the service area.
2. Complete every field: categories, service area, hours, services, and photos. Accuracy
   and completeness drive ranking.
3. Set the booking or call-to-action path so a map view converts to a booking.
4. Mirror the same name, address, and phone across every directory (NAP consistency).
5. Set up the review-request flow so each completed job asks for a review (the main
   local-ranking lever; see local-reviews-reputation).

## Output

A `local_presence`: the verified profile, the categories and service area, the booking
path, and the NAP-consistent directory list.

## Rules

- Completeness and consistency beat tricks. Fill every field and keep NAP identical.
- A local service does not need a national website before it needs this.
